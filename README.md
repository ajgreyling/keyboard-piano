# Kind Keys Piano

Knocked out in a few minutes for my five-year-old to play on old laptops while
I work. Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No build step, no dependencies.

He sits at the keyboard and carefully picks notes that almost sound like music.
This gives him real piano samples, a proper keyboard, multi-touch, and a jam mode
that keeps playing in a pleasant direction — so his "music" does not drive me
crazy while I am trying to think.

## Play

Open `index.html` in a browser, press **Start piano**, then use:

```text
A S D F G H J K L ;
```

- **Pentatonic scale**: almost any combination sounds fine
- **Jam on**: continuous backing that follows his lead
- **Multi-touch**: chords on a touchscreen
- **Old machines**: runs on scrap laptops and tired browsers
